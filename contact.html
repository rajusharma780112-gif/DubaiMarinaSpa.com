FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need To Know

Find answers to some of the most common questions about visiting Dubai Marina Spa.

Do I need to book in advance?

Appointments are recommended to ensure your preferred treatment time is available, although walk-ins are welcome whenever possible.

Which massage is best for first-time guests?

Our Aromatherapy Massage and Traditional Thai Massage are both excellent choices for first-time visitors seeking relaxation.

How long are your treatments?

Most massage treatments are available in 60, 90 and 120-minute sessions.

Do you use premium massage oils?

Yes. We use carefully selected massage oils and professional spa products to ensure every treatment delivers a luxurious experience.

Are private treatment rooms available?

Yes. Every guest enjoys a beautifully designed private treatment room for complete comfort and relaxation.

What are your opening hours?

We are open daily from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
