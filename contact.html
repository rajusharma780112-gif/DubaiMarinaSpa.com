FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need To Know

Here are answers to some of the most common questions before your visit.

Do I need to make an appointment?

Appointments are recommended to guarantee your preferred therapist and time slot, although walk-ins are welcome when availability allows.

Which massage should I choose?

If you're unsure, our experienced team will recommend the treatment best suited to your needs when you arrive.

How long are treatments?

Most treatments are available in 60, 90 and 120-minute sessions.

What should I wear?

Comfortable spa attire and towels are provided. Your privacy and comfort are always our priority.

Are appointments available every day?

Yes. We welcome guests daily from 10:00 AM until 11:00 PM.

Do you offer private treatment rooms?

Yes. Every guest enjoys a beautifully designed private room for complete comfort and relaxation.
