Everything You Need To Know

Find answers to the most common questions before your visit.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended to guarantee your preferred time and therapist, although walk-ins are welcome when availability allows.

Which massage should I choose?

If you're unsure, our experienced therapists will recommend the treatment that best suits your needs.

Most massage sessions are available in 60, 90 and 120-minute options.

Do you use premium oils?

Yes. Every treatment includes carefully selected massage oils and luxury skincare products.

Do you have private treatment rooms?

Yes. Every guest enjoys a beautifully designed private massage suite for complete comfort and privacy.

We are open every day from 10:00 AM until 11:00 PM.
