FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need To Know

Find answers to the most common questions before your visit.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended to ensure your preferred therapist and time are available. Walk-ins are welcome when availability permits.

How long are massage sessions?

Most treatments are available in 60, 90 and 120-minute sessions depending on your preference.

Do you offer private treatment rooms?

Yes. Every guest enjoys a beautifully designed private treatment room for complete comfort and privacy.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and most major credit and debit cards.

Can I book for couples?

Yes. Couples massage appointments are available with advance booking.

What are your opening hours?

We are open daily from 10:00 AM until 11:00 PM.
