Frequently Asked Questions

Which massage should I choose?

If you're visiting for the first time, Swedish Massage is perfect for relaxation. Deep Tissue Massage is recommended for muscle tension and chronic pain.

How long are the treatments?

Most treatments are available in 60, 90 and 120-minute sessions depending on your preference.

Can I customize my treatment?

Absolutely. Our therapists will tailor pressure, techniques and focus areas according to your needs.
