Everything You Need To Know

Find answers to the most common questions about our massage and wellness services.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended to ensure your preferred time is available, although walk-ins are welcome when possible.

Which massage is best for first-time visitors?

A relaxing aromatherapy or traditional Thai massage is a great choice for your first visit.

How long are massage sessions?

Most treatments are available in 60, 90 and 120-minute sessions.

Do you use premium oils?

Yes. We use high-quality massage oils and carefully selected skincare products during every treatment.

Are private treatment rooms available?

Yes. Every guest enjoys a comfortable and private treatment room designed for relaxation.
